# Emotional Peaks Criteria

Identify moments of high emotional intensity that resonate with viewers.

Key emotions to look for:
- Excitement and enthusiasm
- Inspiration and motivation
- Surprise and awe
- Frustration and anger (relatable)
- Joy and celebration
- Fear and concern (that gets resolved)
- Empathy and connection

Indicators of emotional peaks:
- Passionate delivery or tone changes
- Personal stories or vulnerable moments
- Triumphant moments or breakthroughs
- Relatable struggles or failures
- Moments of realization or epiphany
- Expressions of strong opinions
- Celebratory language
- Empowering statements

Examples:
- "I was so frustrated until I discovered..."
- "This is the moment everything changed..."
- "I couldn't believe it when..."
- Personal transformation stories
- Overcoming obstacles narratives
- Moments of genuine excitement

Score segments higher if they:
- Show authentic emotion
- Tell a compelling mini-story
- Create emotional resonance
- Have a clear emotional arc
- Are relatable to the target audience
