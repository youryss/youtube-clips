# Value Bombs Criteria

Find segments that deliver concentrated, actionable value or insights.

Key characteristics:
- Specific, actionable advice
- Tactical tips or strategies
- Key insights or realizations
- Step-by-step instructions
- Tools or resources mentioned
- Frameworks or systems explained
- Common mistakes revealed
- Quick wins or hacks

Types of value bombs:
- "The number one thing you need to do is..."
- "Here's exactly how I did it..."
- "The secret to X is actually..."
- "Most people get this wrong..."
- "Here's the framework I use..."
- "This tool changed everything..."
- Specific numbers, metrics, or results
- Before/after comparisons

Look for:
- Concrete examples
- Specific numbers or data
- Tool or resource recommendations
- Clear cause and effect relationships
- Contrarian but valid insights
- Simplified complex concepts
- Immediately applicable advice
- Unique perspectives or approaches

Score segments higher if they:
- Can stand alone as valuable content
- Teach something specific
- Are immediately actionable
- Challenge common assumptions with evidence
- Provide clear ROI or benefits
- Include specific examples or case studies
